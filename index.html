About Us

Broadway Auto Services Auckland is an automotive repair specialist that aim to fix our Auckland customers vehicle's in the fastest, cheapest and most efficient manner. Broadway Auto Services has got high customer satisfaction reviews in the past year's of service and we are willing to keep up that reputation. We provide our mobile services in Northshore, Central Auckland, Mangere, Manukau, East Tamaki, Botany, West Auckland and Pukekoekoe.

Mobile Automotive Repairs

Broadway Auto Services now have a dedicated team that specialize in providing mobile automotive support. We have got fully equiped van's ready to be sent out directly to your place to fix your automotive issues. This means, less time and effort spent by you in bringing your car to a workshop and waiting.

Broadway's Towing Service

If you ever tend to get stuck in a busy car park, have a breakdown in the middle of a busy highway or unfortunately get involved in a car crash, you have nothing to worry about, we have got a vehicle recovery truck to come to your rescue and tow you to safety. Please call 021 894 403 for any towing emergencies.

On-Site Diagnosing

Yes, you've heard that right, we also provide on-site diagnosing, if you have an engine check light warning, we can diagnose your car at your residence and take out fault codes to help solve the issue. Our team have got the latest diagnostic tools on board and with great experience and knowledge, our team will fix your issue.

Heavy Trucks, Plant and Machinery

Broadway also specialize in Heavy Truck, Plant and Machinery, whether it be a basic service, Panel and Paint, Electrical issues, Diff, Clutch, Gear Box, Engine etc. We do them all. Our team are well skilled and have great experience working with heavy class vehicle and machinery.
